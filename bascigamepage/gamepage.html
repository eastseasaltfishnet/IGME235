The Deer

Description

The Deer is a very short educational interactive experience for small children.
The player needs to move a deer left to right around a forest (using the A and D keys) and read snippets of text teaching about the Pleistocene Epoch.
There are three mini-games: a memory game, a bone jigsaw puzzle, and a "click on deer food" quiz.
